Welcome to the UW Medicine COVID-19 SecureLink results portal.

If you were tested for the SARS-CoV-2 (COVID-19) Virus, you can use this portal to retrieve the status and/or results of your testing. Only patients who received a test retrieval card at the time of their test are eligible to retrieve their results via this portal. To retrieve your test result, please enter the 16 digit retrieval code and your date of birth in the form below.

If you are a SCAN study participant and have an 8-digit code, please use the SCAN results portal to retreive your result.
